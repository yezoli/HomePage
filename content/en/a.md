### About me
Zonglin Ye (Graduate Student Member, IEEE) was born in Suzhou, Jiangsu, China, in 2000. He received the B.E. degree in microelectronics from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China, in 2022, where he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree. His research interests include fractional-N frequency synthesizers and high-performance oscillators.
